Who visits, and where their attention goes.

Unique viewers per page and the real time — visible-tab only — spent in each section. No cookies; visitors are counted by a first-party id.

loading…

Time is attention time: it only accrues while the browser tab is visible, and is credited to whichever section is on screen (or the active view, inside the Resilience Intelligence System). “Avg / view” divides a section’s total time by that page’s number of loads. Unique viewers are de-duplicated per page by a durable first-party id stored in the visitor’s browser — clearing site data or a fresh browser counts as new.
