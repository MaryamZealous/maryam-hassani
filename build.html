Resilience Intelligence System

An illustrative instrument for national resilience. It separates a country’s structural fundamentals from its live, day-to-day stress — and reads the gap between them as the early-warning signal.

Built on real public feeds — satellite strait transits, world news, sea state, sanctions and markets — with every number one click from its formula and source. Not an official assessment; a working example of what you can build, usefully, right now.
